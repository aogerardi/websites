Our Story

From a single mower to the whole block.

Greenhaven started in 2009 when our founder, Marcus, bought a used mower and knocked on doors around his Naperville neighborhood. One tidy lawn led to another, and word spread the old-fashioned way.

Fifteen years later we're a full crew caring for hundreds of properties across the western suburbs — but we still run the business the way Marcus started it: be honest, be on time, and take pride in every yard.

We're fully licensed and insured, and we treat your property with the same care we'd give our own.
