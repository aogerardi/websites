Our Story

A Taste of Home on Glendale Ave

Casa Del Taco started with a simple idea: serve the food we grew up on, made the way it's supposed to be — carne asada grilled to order, tortillas pressed by hand, and salsas nobody buys in a jar.

Years later, our regulars still line up for the same carne asada street tacos, weekend menudo, and the kind of welcome that turns first-timers into familia.

— The Casa Del Taco Family
