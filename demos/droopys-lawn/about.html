Our Story

From a single mower to the whole block.

Droopy's started in 2016 when our founder, John, bought a used mower and knocked on doors around his Muncie neighborhood. One tidy lawn led to another, and word spread the old-fashioned way.

Fifteen years later we're a full crew caring for hundreds of properties across the East Central Indiana — but we still run the business the way John started it: be honest, be on time, and take pride in every yard.

We're fully licensed and insured, and we treat your property with the same care we'd give our own.
