Frequently Asked

The questions we hear most.

How much does a website cost?

Most projects start from $250, agreed as a flat rate up front. More involved builds (online booking, stores) are quoted individually.

How long does it take?

A typical site is ready in 1–2 weeks, depending on how quickly we get your content (text, photos, logo) and feedback.

What do you need from me to start?

A rough idea of what you want, any existing branding or photos, and your business details. If you don't have content ready, we'll help you put it together.

Are there ongoing costs?

Only hosting and your domain — typically $10–$20/month, billed at cost by the provider. The design fee is one-time.

Can you update my site later?

Yes. Small tweaks are quick and cheap, and we can set up an ongoing arrangement if you'd like regular updates.

Do you only work with certain industries?

No. We have ready-made templates for trades like lawncare and pools, but we build custom sites for any small business.

Why “Piedmondo”?

It’s Italian for “foot of the world” (piede + mondo) — a nod to grounded, down-to-earth work that still reaches anyone, anywhere. Big-business quality at a small-business price.
